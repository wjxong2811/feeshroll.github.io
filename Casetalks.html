Case Talks

Case Talks is an online seminar for the discussion of criminal law cases, bringing together criminal law scholars from the Anglosphere and Germany, organised with Johannes Weigel (Göttingen and Max Planck Institute for the Study of Crime, Security and Law).

We discuss one criminal case each session. No registration is required: feel free to join us.
